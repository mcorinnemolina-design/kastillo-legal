This practice is not a law firm, and the services provided are not a substitute for legal services that must be performed by a lawyer.

Use of this website, email communications, or booking an appointment does not create a lawyer–client relationship.

Services are provided by a licensed paralegal and notary public, regulated by the Law Society of Ontario, and are limited to matters authorized under Ontario paralegal regulations. Where a matter falls outside this scope, services may be declined and a referral may be suggested.
